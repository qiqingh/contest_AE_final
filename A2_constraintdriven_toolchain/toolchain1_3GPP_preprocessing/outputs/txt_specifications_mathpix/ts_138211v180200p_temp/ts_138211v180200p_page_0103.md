6.4.1.2 Phase-tracking reference signals for PUSCH
6.4.1.2.1 Sequence generation
6.4.1.2.1.1 Sequence generation if transform precoding is not enabled

If transform precoding is not enabled, the precoded phase-tracking reference signal for subcarrier \( k \) on layer \( j \) is given by
\[
r^{\left(\tilde{p_{j}}\right)}(m)=\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
r(m) & \text { if } j=j^{\prime} \text { or } j=j^{\prime \prime} \\
0 & \text { otherwise }
\end{array}\right.
\]
where
- antenna ports \( \tilde{p}_{I^{\prime}} \) or \( \left\{\tilde{p}_{J^{\prime}}, \tilde{p}_{J^{\prime \prime}}\right\} \) associated with PT-RS transmission are given by clause 6.2 .3 of [6, TS 38.214]
- \( r(m) \) is given by clause 6.4.1.1.1.1
- at the position of the first DM-RS symbol in absence of PUSCH intra-slot frequency hopping
- at the position of the first DM-RS symbol in hop \( h \in\{0,1\} \) in presence of PUSCH intra-slot frequency hopping
6.4.1.2.1.2 Sequence generation if transform precoding is enabled

If transform precoding is enabled, the phase-tracking reference signal \( r_{m}\left(m^{\prime}\right) \) to be mapped in position \( m \) before transform precoding, where \( m \) depends on the number of PT-RS groups \( N_{\text {group }}^{\text {PT-RS }} \), the number of samples per PT-RS group \( N_{\text {samp }}^{\text {group }} \), and \( M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUSCH}} \) according to Table 6.4.1.2.2.2-1, shall be generated according to
\[
\begin{aligned}
r_{m}\left(m^{\prime}\right) & =w\left(k^{\prime}\right) \frac{e^{j \frac{\pi}{2}(m \bmod 2)}}{\sqrt{2}}\left[\left(1-2 c\left(m^{\prime}\right)\right)+j\left(1-2 c\left(m^{\prime}\right)\right)\right] \\
m^{\prime} & =N_{\mathrm{samp}}^{\text {goup }} s^{\prime}+k^{\prime} \\
s^{\prime} & =0,1, \ldots, N_{\text {goup }}^{\text {PT-RS }}-1 \\
k^{\prime} & =0,1, N_{\mathrm{samp}}^{\text {group }}-1
\end{aligned}
\]
where the pseudo-random sequence \( c(i) \) is defined in clause 5.2.1 and \( w(i) \) is given by Table 6.4.1.2.1.2-1. The pseudo-random sequence generator shall be initialized with
\[
c_{\mathrm{init}}=\left(2^{17}\left(N_{\mathrm{symb}}^{\text {slot }} n_{\mathrm{s}, \mathrm{f}}^{\mu}+l+1\right)\left(2 N_{\mathrm{JD}}+1\right)+2 N_{\mathrm{ID}}\right) \bmod 2^{31}
\]
