For the purpose of PT-RS mapping, the resource blocks allocated for PSSCH transmission are numbered from 0 to \( N_{\mathrm{RB}}-1 \) from the lowest scheduled resource block to the highest. The corresponding subcarriers in this set of resource blocks are numbered in increasing order starting from the lowest frequency from 0 to \( N_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{RB}} N_{\mathrm{RB}}-1 \). The subcarriers to which the PT-RS shall be mapped are given by
\[
\begin{aligned}
k & =k_{\mathrm{ref}}^{\mathrm{RE}}+\left(i K_{\mathrm{PT}-\mathrm{RS}}+k_{\mathrm{ref}}^{\mathrm{RB}}\right) N_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{RB}} \\
k_{\mathrm{ref}}^{\mathrm{RB}} & =\left\{\begin{array}{ll}
N_{\mathrm{ID}} \bmod K_{\mathrm{PT}-\mathrm{RS}} & \text { if } N_{\mathrm{RB}} \bmod K_{\mathrm{PT}-\mathrm{RS}}=0 \\
N_{\mathrm{ID}} \bmod \left(N_{\mathrm{RB}} \bmod K_{\mathrm{PT}-\mathrm{RS}}\right) & \text { otherwise }
\end{array}\right.
\end{aligned}
\]
where
- \( i=0,1,2, \ldots \)
- \( \quad k_{\mathrm{ref}}^{\mathrm{RE}} \) is given by Table 8.4.1.2.2-1 for the DM-RS port associated with the PT-RS port according to clause 8.2.3 in [6, TS 38.214].
- \( \quad N_{\mathrm{RB}} \) is the number of resource blocks scheduled;
- \( K_{\text {PT-RS }} \in\{2,4\} \) is given by [6, TS 38.214];

A UE is not expected to receive sidelink CSI-RS and PSSCH PT-RS on the same resource elements.

8.4.1.3 Demodulation reference signals for PSCCH
8.4.1.3.1 Sequence generation

The sequence \( r_{l}(m) \) shall be generated according to
\[
r_{l}(m)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(1-2 c(2 m))+j \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(1-2 c(2 m+1))
\]
where the pseudo-random sequence \( c(m) \) is defined in clause 5.2.1. The pseudo-random sequence generator shall be initialized with
\[
c_{\mathrm{init}}=\left(2^{17}\left(N_{\mathrm{symb}}^{\mathrm{slot}} n_{\mathrm{s}, \mathrm{f}}^{\mu}+l+1\right)\left(2 N_{\mathrm{ID}}+1\right)+2 N_{\mathrm{ID}}\right) \bmod 2^{31}
\]
where
- \( l \) is the OFDM symbol number within the slot,
- \( n_{\mathrm{s}, \mathrm{f}}^{\mu} \) is the slot number within a frame, and
- \( \quad N_{I D} \in\{0,1, \ldots, 65535\} \) is given by the higher-layer parameter s/-DMRS-ScramblelD.
