Base sequences \( \bar{r}_{u, v}(n) \) are divided into groups, where \( u \in\{0,1, \ldots, 29\} \) is the group number and \( v \) is the base sequence number within the group, such that each group contains one base sequence \( (v=0) \) of each length \( M_{\mathrm{ZC}}=m N_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{RB}} / 2^{\delta} \), \( 1 / 2 \leq m / 2^{\delta} \leq 5 \) and two base sequences ( \( v=0,1 \) ) of each length \( M_{\mathrm{ZC}}=m N_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{RB}} / 2^{\delta}, 6 \leq m / 2^{\delta} \). The definition of the base sequence \( \bar{r}_{u, v}(0), \ldots, \bar{r}_{u, v}\left(M_{Z C}-1\right) \) depends on the sequence length \( M_{Z C} \) -
5.2.2.1 Base sequences of length 36 or larger

For \( M_{\mathrm{ZC}} \geq 3 N_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{RB}} \), the base sequence \( \bar{r}_{u, \nu}(0), \ldots, \bar{r}_{u, \nu}\left(M_{\mathrm{ZC}}-1\right) \) is given by
\[
\begin{array}{l}
\bar{r}_{w, v}(n)=x_{q}\left(n \bmod N_{Z C}\right) \\
x_{q}(m)=e^{-j \frac{\pi m(m+1)}{N_{Z C}}}
\end{array}
\]
where
\[
\begin{array}{l}
q=\lfloor\bar{q}+1 / 2\rfloor+v \cdot(-1)^{\lfloor 2 \bar{q}\rfloor} \\
\bar{q}=N_{\mathrm{ZC}} \cdot(u+1) / 31
\end{array}
\]

The length \( N_{Z C} \) is given by the largest prime number such that \( N_{Z C}<M_{Z C} \) -
5.2.2.2 Base sequences of length less than 36

For \( M_{Z C} \in\{6,12,18,24\} \) the base sequence is given by
\[
\bar{r}_{u, v}(n)=e^{j \varphi(n) \pi / 4}, \quad 0 \leq n \leq M_{\mathrm{ZC}}-1
\]
where the value of \( \varphi(n) \) is given by Tables 5.2.2.2-1 to 5.2.2.2-4.
For \( M_{Z C}=30 \), the base sequence \( \bar{r}_{u, v}(0), \ldots, \bar{r}_{u, v}\left(M_{Z C}-1\right) \) is given by
\[
\bar{r}_{w, v}(n)=e^{-j \frac{\pi(w+l)(n+l)(n+2)}{3 l}}, 0 \leq n \leq M_{Z C}-1
\]
