6.3.2.6.2 Modulation

The block of scrambled bits \( \tilde{b}(0), \ldots, \tilde{b}\left(M_{\text {bit }}-1\right) \) shall be modulated as described in clause 5.1 using QPSK unless \( \pi / 2 \) BPSK is configured, resulting in a block of complex-valued modulation symbols \( d(0), \ldots, d\left(M_{\text {symb }}-1\right) \) where \( M_{\text {symb }}=M_{\text {bit }} / 2 \) for QPSK and \( M_{\text {symb }}=M_{\text {bit }} \) for \( \pi / 2 \)-BPSK.
6.3.2.6.3 Block-wise spreading

For both PUCCH format 3 and \( 4, M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s}=M_{\mathrm{RB}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s} N_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{RB}} \) with \( M_{\mathrm{RB}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH} s} \) representing the bandwidth of the PUCCH in terms of resource blocks according to clauses 9.2.3,9.2.5.1 and 9.2.5.2 of [5, TS 38.213] and shall for non-interlaced mapping fulfil
\[
M_{\mathrm{RB}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s}=2^{\alpha_{2}} \cdot 3^{\alpha_{3}} \cdot 5^{\alpha_{5}}
\]
where \( \alpha_{2}, \alpha_{3}, \alpha_{s} \) is a set of non-negative integers and \( s \in\{3,4\} \). For interlaced mapping, \( M_{\mathrm{RB}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, 3}=10 \) if a single interlace is configured and \( M_{\mathrm{RB}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, 3}=20 \) if two interlaces are configured.

For PUCCH format 3, if interlaced mapping is not configured, no block-wise spreading is applied and
\[
\begin{aligned}
y\left(\mid M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, 3}+k\right) & =d\left(\mid M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, 3}+k\right) \\
k & =0,1, \ldots, M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, 3}-1 \\
l & =0,1, \ldots,\left(M_{\mathrm{symb}} / M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, 3}\right)-1
\end{aligned}
\]
where \( M_{\mathrm{RB}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH} 3} \geq 1 \) is given by clauses 9.2.3,9.2.5.1 and 9.2.5.2 of [5, TS 38.213] and \( N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH} 3}=1 \).
For PUCCH format 3 with interlaced mapping and PUCCH format 4 , block-wise spreading shall be applied according to
\[
\begin{aligned}
y\left(l M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s}+k\right) & =w_{n}\left(\left\lfloor k \frac{N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s}}{M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s}}\right\rfloor\right) d\left(l \frac{M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s}}{N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s}}+k \bmod \frac{M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s}}{N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s}}\right) \\
k & =0,1, \ldots, M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s}-1 \\
l & =0,1, \ldots,\left(N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s} M_{\mathrm{symb}} / M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s}\right)-1
\end{aligned}
\]
where
- for PUCCH format 3 with interlaced mapping, \( N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, 3} \in\{1,2,4\} \) if a single interlace is configured and \( N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} 3}=1, w_{n}=1 \) if two interlaces are configured;
- for PUCCH format 4, \( M_{\text {RB }}^{\text {PUCCH } 4} \) is given by clause 9.2.1 of [5, TS 38.213] and \( N_{\text {SF }}^{\text {PUCCH } 4} \in\{2,4\} \) is given by the higher-layer parameter occ-Length;
and \( w_{A} \) is given by Tables 6.3.2.6.3-1 and 6.3.2.6.3-2 for \( N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}} s>1 \) where \( n \) is the index of the orthogonal sequence to use according to clause 9.2.1 of [5, TS 38.213]. The quantity \( N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{3}} \in\{2,4\} \) is given by the higher-layer parameter occ-Length if provided, otherwise \( N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH} 3}=1 \).
