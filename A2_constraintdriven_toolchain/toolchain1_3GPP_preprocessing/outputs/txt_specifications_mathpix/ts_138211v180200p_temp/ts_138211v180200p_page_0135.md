7.4.1.3 Demodulation reference signals for PDCCH
7.4.1.3.1 Sequence generation

The UE shall assume the reference-signal sequence \( r_{l}(m) \) for OFDM symbol \( l \) is defined by
\[
\eta_{l}(m)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(1-2 \cdot c(2 m))+j \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(1-2 \cdot c(2 m+1))
\]
where the pseudo-random sequence \( c(i) \) is defined in clause 5.2.1. The pseudo-random sequence generator shall be initialized with
\[
c_{\text {init }}=\left(2^{17}\left(N_{\text {symb }}^{\text {slot }} n_{\mathrm{s}, \mathrm{f}}^{\mu}+l+1\right)\left(2 N_{\mathrm{ID}}+1\right)+2 N_{\mathrm{ID}}\right) \bmod 2^{31}
\]
where \( l \) is the OFDM symbol number within the slot, \( n_{\mathrm{s}, \mathrm{f}}^{\mu} \) is the slot number within a frame, and
- \( \quad N_{\text {ID }} \in\{0,1, \ldots, 65535\} \) is given by the higher-layer parameter pdcch-DMRS-ScramblingID if provided;
