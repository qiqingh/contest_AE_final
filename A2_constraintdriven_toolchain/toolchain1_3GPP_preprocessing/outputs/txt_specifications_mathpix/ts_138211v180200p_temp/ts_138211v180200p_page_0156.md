8.4.1.1.2 Mapping to physical resources

The sequence \( r(m) \) shall be mapped to the intermediate quantity \( \tilde{a}_{k, l}^{\left(\tilde{p}_{j}, \mu\right)} \) according to clause 6.4.1.1.3 using configuration type 1 without transform precoding, and where \( w_{\mathrm{f}}\left(k^{\prime}\right), w_{\mathrm{t}}\left(l^{\prime}\right) \), and \( \Delta \) are given by Table 8.4.1.1.2-2, and \( r(m) \) is specified in clause 8.4.1.1.1.

The patterns used for the PSSCH DM-RS is indicated in the SCI as described in clause 8.3.1.1 of [4, TS 38.212].
The intermediate quantity \( \tilde{a}_{k, l}^{\left(\tilde{p}_{j, \mu}\right)} \) shall be precoded, multiplied with the amplitude scaling factor \( \beta_{\text {DMRS }}^{\text {PSSCH }} \) specified in clause 8.3.1.5, and mapped to physical resources according to
\[
\left[\begin{array}{c}
a_{k, l}^{\left(p_{0,} \mu\right)} \\
\vdots \\
a_{k, l}^{\left(p_{\rho-1}, \mu\right)}
\end{array}\right]=\beta_{\mathrm{DMRS}}^{\mathrm{PSSCH}} W\left[\begin{array}{c}
\tilde{a}_{k, l}^{\left(\tilde{p}_{0,} \mu\right)} \\
\vdots \\
\tilde{a}_{k, l}^{\left(\tilde{p}_{v-1}, \mu\right)}
\end{array}\right]
\]
where
- the precoding matrix \( W \) is given by clause 8.3.1.4,
- the set of antenna ports \( \left\{p_{0}, \ldots, p_{\rho-1}\right\} \) is given by clause 8.3.1.4, and
- the set of antenna ports \( \left\{\tilde{p}_{0}, \ldots, \tilde{p}_{v-1}\right\} \) is given by [6, TS 38.214];
and the following conditions are fulfilled:
- the resource elements \( \tilde{a}_{k, l}^{\left(\tilde{p}_{j} \mu\right)} \) are within the common resource blocks allocated for PSSCH transmission.

The quantity \( k \) is defined relative to subcarrier 0 in common resource block 0 and the quantity \( l \) is defined relative to the start of the scheduled resources for transmission of PSSCH and the associated PSCCH, including the OFDM symbol duplicated as described in clauses 8.3.1.5 and 8.3.2.3.

The position(s) of the DM-RS symbols is given by \( \bar{l} \) according to Table 8.4.1.1.2-1 where the number of PSSCH DMRS is indicated in the SCI, and \( l_{\mathrm{d}} \) is the duration of the scheduled resources for transmission of PSSCH according to clause 8.1.2.1 of [6, TS 38.214] and the associated PSCCH, including the OFDM symbol duplicated as described in clauses 8.3.1.5 and 8.3.2.3.
