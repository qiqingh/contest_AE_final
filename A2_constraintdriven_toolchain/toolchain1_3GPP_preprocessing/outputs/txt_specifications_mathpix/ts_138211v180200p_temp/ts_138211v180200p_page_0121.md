7.3.1.3 Layer mapping

The UE shall assume that complex-valued modulation symbols for each of the codewords to be transmitted are mapped onto one or several layers according to Table 7.3.1.3-1. Complex-valued modulation symbols \( d^{(q)}(0), \ldots, d^{(q)}\left(M_{\text {symb }}^{(q)}-\right. \) 1) for codeword \( q \) shall be mapped onto the layers \( x(i)=\left[x^{(0)}(i) \quad \ldots \quad x^{(v-1)}(i)\right]^{\mathrm{T}}, i=0,1, \ldots, M_{\text {symb }}^{\text {layer }}-1 \) where \( v \) is the number of layers and \( M_{\text {symb }}^{\text {layer }} \) is the number of modulation symbols per layer.
