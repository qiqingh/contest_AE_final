When SRS is transmitted on a given SRS resource, the sequence \( r^{\left(p_{i}\right)}\left(n, l^{\prime}\right) \) for each OFDM symbol \( l^{\prime} \) and for each of the antenna ports of the SRS resource shall be multiplied with the amplitude scaling factor \( \beta_{\mathrm{SRS}} \) in order to conform to the transmit power specified in [5,38-213] and mapped in sequence starting with \( r^{\left(p_{j}\right)}\left(0, l^{\prime}\right) \) to resource elements \( (k, l) \) in a slot for each of the antenna ports \( p_{i} \) according to
\[
a_{K_{\mathrm{Tc}} k^{\prime}+k_{0}^{\left(p_{i}\right)},_{l^{\prime}+l_{0}}^{\left(p_{i}\right)}}=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}
\frac{1}{\sqrt{N_{\mathrm{ap}}}} \beta_{\mathrm{SRS}} r^{\left(p_{i}\right)}\left(k^{\prime}, l^{\prime}\right) & \text { if } k^{\prime}=0,1, \ldots, M_{\mathrm{sc}, b}^{\mathrm{SRS}}-1 \text { and } l^{\prime}=0,1, \ldots, N_{\mathrm{symb}}^{\mathrm{SRS}}-1 \\
0 & \text { otherwise }
\end{array}\right.
\]

The length of the sounding reference signal sequence is given by
\[
M_{\mathrm{sc}, b}^{\mathrm{SRS}}=m_{\mathrm{SRS}, b} N_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{RB}} /\left(K_{\mathrm{TC}} P_{\mathrm{F}}\right)
\]
where \( m_{\mathrm{SRS}, b} \) is given by a selected row of Table 6.4.1.4.3-1 with \( b=B_{\mathrm{SRS}} \) where \( B_{\mathrm{SRS}} \in\{0,1,2,3\} \) is given by the field \( b \)-SRS contained in the higher-layer parameter freqHopping if configured, otherwise \( B_{\text {SRS }}=0 \). The row of the table is selected according to the index \( C_{\mathrm{SRS}} \in\{0,1, \ldots, 63\} \) given by the field \( c \)-SRS contained in the higher-layer parameter freqHopping. The quantity \( P_{F} \in\{2,4\} \) is given by the higher-layer parameter FreqScalingFactor if configured, otherwise \( P_{F}=1 \). When FreqScalingFactor is configured, the UE expects the length of the SRS sequence to be a multiple of 6 .

The frequency-domain starting position \( k_{0}^{\left(p_{i}\right)} \) is defined by
\[
k_{0}^{\left(p_{i}\right)}=\bar{k}_{0}^{\left(p_{i}\right)}+n_{\text {offset }}^{\mathrm{FH}}+n_{\text {offset }}^{\mathrm{RPFS}}+n_{\text {offset } 2}^{\mathrm{FH}}
\]
where
\[
\bar{k}_{0}^{\left(p_{i}\right)}=n_{\mathrm{shift}} N_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{RB}}+\left(k_{\mathrm{TC}}^{\left(p_{i}\right)}+k_{\mathrm{offset}}^{l^{\prime}}+f_{\mathrm{coh}}\left(n_{\mathrm{f}}, n_{\mathrm{s}, \mathrm{f}}^{\mu}, l^{\prime \prime}\right)\right) \bmod K_{\mathrm{TC}}
\]
and
\[
\begin{array}{l} 
\\
n_{\text {offset }}^{\text {RPFS }}=N_{\text {sc }}^{\text {RB }} m_{\text {SRS } B_{\text {SRS }}}\left(\left(k_{\mathrm{F}}+k_{\text {hop }}\right) \bmod P_{\mathrm{F}}\right) / P_{\mathrm{F}} \\
n_{\text {offset2 }}^{\mathrm{FH}}=\left(\left(n_{\text {init }}^{\mathrm{hop}}+n_{\mathrm{SRS}}^{\mathrm{TxH} \text { opping }}\right) \bmod N_{\text {hop }}-n_{\text {init }}^{\text {hop }}\right)\left(m_{\mathrm{SRS}, 0}-m_{\text {overlap }}^{\text {hop }}\right) N_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{RB}}
\end{array}
\]
and
- \( k_{\mathrm{F}} \in\left\{0,1, \ldots, P_{\mathrm{F}}-1\right\} \) is given by the higher-layer parameter StartRBIndex if configured, otherwise \( k_{\mathrm{F}}=0 \);
- \( k_{\text {hop }} \) is given by Table 6.4.1.4.3-3 with
\[
\begin{aligned}
\bar{k}_{\mathrm{hop}} & =\left\lfloor\frac{n_{\mathrm{SRS}}}{\prod_{b \prime=b_{\mathrm{hop}}}^{B_{\mathrm{SRS}}} N_{b}}\right\rfloor \bmod P_{\mathrm{F}} \\
N_{b_{\mathrm{hop}}} & =1
\end{aligned}
\]
