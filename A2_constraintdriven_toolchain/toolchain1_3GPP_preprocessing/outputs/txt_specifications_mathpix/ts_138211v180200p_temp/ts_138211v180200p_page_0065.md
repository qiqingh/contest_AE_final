6.3.2.4.2 Mapping to physical resources

The sequence \( z(n) \) shall be multiplied with the amplitude scaling factor \( \beta_{\text {PUCCH } 1} \) in order to conform to the transmit power specified in [5, TS 38.213] and mapped in sequence starting with \( z(n) \) to resource elements \( (k, l)_{p, \mu} \) which meet all of the following criteria:
- they are in the resource blocks assigned for transmission,
- they are not used by the associated DM-RS

The mapping to resource elements \( (k, l)_{p, \mu} \) not reserved for other purposes shall be in increasing order of first the index \( k \) over the assigned physical resource blocks according to clause 9.2.1 of [5, TS 38.213], and then the index \( l \) on antenna port \( p=2000 \).

For interlaced transmission, the mapping operation shall be repeated for each resource block in the interlace and in the active bandwidth part over the assigned physical resource blocks according to clause 9.21 of [5, TS 38.213], with the resource-block dependent sequence generated according to clause 6.3.2.2.
