6.3.2.6.4 Transform precoding

The block of complex-valued symbols \( y(0), \ldots, y\left(N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}_{1} s} M_{\mathrm{symb}}-1\right) \) shall be transform precoded according to
\[
\begin{aligned}
z\left(l \cdot M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s}+k\right) & =\frac{1}{\sqrt{M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s}}} \sum_{m=0}^{M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s}-1} y\left(l \cdot M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s}+m\right) e^{-j \frac{2 \pi n k}{M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s}}} \\
k & =0, \ldots, M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s}-1 \\
l & =0, \ldots,\left(N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s} M_{\mathrm{symb}} / M_{\mathrm{sc}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s}\right)-1
\end{aligned}
\]
resulting in a block of complex-valued symbols \( z(0), \ldots, z\left(N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s} M_{\mathrm{symb}}-1\right) \).
6.3.2.6.5 Mapping to physical resources

The block of modulation symbols \( z(0), \ldots, z\left(N_{\mathrm{SF}}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s} M_{\mathrm{symb}}-1\right) \) shall be multiplied with the amplitude scaling factor \( \beta_{\text {PUCCH,s }} \) in order to conform to the transmit power specified in [5, TS 38.213] and mapped in sequence starting with \( z(0) \) to resource elements \( (k, l)_{p, \mu} \) which meet all of the following criteria:
- they are in the resource blocks assigned for transmission,
- they are not used by the associated DM-RS

The mapping to resource elements \( (k, l)_{p, \mu} \) not reserved for other purposes shall be in increasing order of first the index \( k \) over the assigned physical resource blocks according to clause 9.2.1 of [5, TS 38.213], and then the index \( l \) on antenna port \( p=2000 \).

In case of intra-slot frequency hopping according to clause 9.2 .1 of [5, TS 38.213], \( \left\lfloor N_{\text {symb }}^{\text {PUCCH,s }} / 2\right\rfloor \) OFDM symbols shall be transmitted in the first hop and \( N_{\text {symb }}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s}-\left\lfloor N_{\text {symb }}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s} / 2\right\rfloor \) symbols in the second hop where \( N_{\text {symb }}^{\mathrm{PUCCH}, s} \) is the total number of OFDM symbols used in one slot for PUCCH transmission.
